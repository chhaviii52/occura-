HILLFAIR CULTURAL FEST

Hill'ffair, the annual cultural fest of NIT Hamirpur which is around the corner, promises to be an immersive experience into the maritime legends and adventures.A festival or cultural event is a celebration that focuses upon a theme and may run for hours to weeks.

NIMBUS TECHNICAL FEST

Nimbus is a technical fest of North India. It is all about students from different branches coming together, Forming departmental teams and societies, making technical projects and organizing workshops and exhibitions. Nimbus is a chance to present your innovation, creativity and promptness.

DEPARTMENTAL SOCITIES

NIT Hamirpur has always striven to encourage an equitable balance between academics and extracurricular engagements among its students. Inorder to promote this various societies have been encouraged under various depatments and technical organisations.
