NATI CLUB

Nati is the most famous dance of Himachal Pradesh. It is listed in the Guinness world record book as the largest folk dance.The word Nati is used for the traditional folk songs sung in the Western and Central Hills of the Indian subcontinent. It is primarily native to the state of Himachal Pradesh.
